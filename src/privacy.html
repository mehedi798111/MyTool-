Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MyTool is designed to be privacy-first. Most of our tools process data locally in your browser. We do not store your personal inputs on our servers. The only data we may collect is anonymized usage statistics to improve our service.

2. Local Storage

Tools like the To-Do List and Habit Tracker use your browser's Local Storage to save your progress. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

3. Third-Party Services

We use standard web analytics and font providers (Google Fonts) which may collect basic device information as part of their standard service delivery.

4. Contact Information

If you contact us through our contact form, we will use your email only to reply to your inquiry.
