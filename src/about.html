About MyTool

MyTool is a comprehensive, free-to-use platform that brings together over 50 essential tools for developers, designers, writers, and students. Our mission is to simplify your digital workflow by providing fast, reliable, and privacy-focused utilities in one place.

Whether you need to format JSON, calculate your BMI, or generate a secure password, MyTool has you covered. Everything runs right in your browser, meaning your data stays with you.

Why Choose MyTool?

  • Always Free: No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no limits.
  • Privacy First: We don't store your sensitive data. Processing happens locally on your device.
  • Responsive: Use our tools on your phone, tablet, or desktop.
